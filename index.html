Card Condition Standards

Please note: Keeping in mind the subjectivity of grading, adherence to a generally accepted standard has tried to been applied when determining the condition of the card.

Near Mint

Will show minimal to zero wear, in addition to fresh, crisp corners and clean edges with essentially zero flaws.

A Near Mint card may have a tiny edge chip or a couple tiny scratches, but overall looks un-played.

Lightly Played

Cards may have light outer edge or corner wear or even just light scratches or scuffs.

There are no major issues with the card structure itself, for example liquid damage, bends or creases, or tears of any kind.

Moderately Played

Cards can have worn corners and borders, scratches, creases or chipping of the edges or any combination of these, but if that is the case these issues must be slight.

Creasing must not affect the structural integrity of the card.
